## ЁЯзН The Story Behind Habbit

> _"I'll wake up at 6AM tomorrow."_
> _"I'll start reading tonight."_
> _"I'll finally build that workout routine... next Monday."_

Sound familiar? That's basically my entire life for the past 3 years.

I'm a developer. I build things for a living. I solve problems, write systems, architect solutions тАФ but I could **never** hold myself accountable to a simple morning routine. The irony wasn't lost on me.

I tried every habit app out there. They were either:

- ЁЯРМ **Too slow** тАФ opening to a loading screen every morning killed my momentum
- ЁЯМР **Cloud-dependent** тАФ needing Wi-Fi just to mark a checkbox? No thanks.
- ЁЯУК **Too complex** тАФ I wanted to track habits, not get a psychology degree
- ЁЯСБя╕П **Too invasive** тАФ sending my data to servers I don't know, for purposes I didn't agree to

So one weekend, fueled by cold coffee and mild self-loathing, I opened VS Code and started building **Habbit** тАФ a habit tracker made by someone who desperately needed one.

This app is my accountability partner. Maybe it'll be yours too.

## ЁЯФФ Notifications That Respect You

Most apps use push notifications routed through their servers тАФ which means they know when you use the app, what habits you have, and when you're active.

Habbit does it differently.

All reminders are **local notifications** тАФ scheduled entirely on your device, triggered by your device's clock, with zero server involvement.

```
ЁЯУ╡  No push notification server
ЁЯМР  Works in airplane mode
ЁЯСБя╕П  We never see when or if you open a reminder
ЁЯФХ  You control them тАФ snooze, dismiss, or turn off anytime
тЪЩя╕П  Per-habit scheduling тАФ set different times for different habits
```

> _Your 6AM workout reminder is between you and your phone. Nobody else._

## тЪб Performance Philosophy

Here's the thing about habit apps тАФ you open them **every single morning**. Sometimes half-asleep. Sometimes already running late.

The last thing you need is a 3-second splash screen.

That's why Habbit uses **[MMKV](https://github.com/mrousavy/react-native-mmkv)** тАФ a high-performance key-value storage library originally built at WeChat, handling millions of users. It's:

- ЁЯПОя╕П **~30x faster** than AsyncStorage
- ЁЯУж **Tiny** тАФ adds minimal size to the app bundle
- тЪЩя╕П **Synchronous** тАФ no async/await, no Promise chains, just instant reads
- ЁЯФЛ **Battery friendly** тАФ efficient I/O means less CPU wake time

Your habits load instantly. Your streak updates instantly. Your morning stays uninterrupted.
